# Use Stripe Separate Charges and Transfers

Miso will use Stripe separate charges and transfers for marketplace payments instead of destination charges. Destination charges fit one buyer and one seller, but Miso resale can split one buyer payment between the resale seller, the event organizer through an organizer resale royalty, and Miso through the marketplace fee. Separate charges and transfers keep one card payment on the platform account while allowing multiple connected-account transfers after ticket fulfillment is confirmed.
